It's All Good is the ninth album released by MC Breed . It was released on February 9, 1999 for Power Records , distributed by Roadrunner Records and was produced by MC Breed and Ricco Lumpkins. After seven years with Wrap Records , this MC Breed's first album with Power Records. Breed returned to the Billboard 200 with this album, as the album peaked at #180 on the chart as well as #41 on the Top R&B/Hip-Hop Albums .
